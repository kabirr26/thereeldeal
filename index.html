The Big Sick Review

By Kabir Rao

From July 29, 2017

From the moment I heard word of Kumail Nanjiani’s original film, The Big Sick, I was hooked, having loved Kumail’s performance on HBO’s Silicon Valley. Then, the first trailer dropped and I knew that I would be seeing this film the weekend it expanded to a wide release. Now, having seen it, I am so happy to say that The Big Sick is one of the, if not the, best romantic comedies of the last decade.

Wonder Woman Review

By Kabir Rao

From June 16, 2017

While it gets very emotional at times, Wonder Woman never feels like it is taking itself too seriously not does it fall into the same tropes that we are beginning to observe in most other superhero films and transcends the genre, similar to Logan. A film that will be loved by all audiences alike, Wonder Woman is groundbreaking and one that we will cherish for a long time to come.

La La Land Review

By Kabir Rao

From January 21, 2017

Who knew Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling could sing? This December, Whiplash director Damien Chazelle brought us the highly anticipated, feel-good movie of the year, La La Land, now the messiah of the musical genre. This film was everything it was hyped up to be and then some.
